Acme Corp — Customer Support Policy

REFUND POLICY

Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase. To initiate a refund, the customer must provide the order number and a brief reason for the request. Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days and returned to the original payment method.

Damaged or defective items are eligible for an immediate replacement or full refund regardless of the 30-day window. Customers must provide photographic evidence of the damage when submitting a refund request for damaged goods.

Digital products and downloadable items are non-refundable once accessed. Subscription plans can be cancelled at any time but are non-refundable for the current billing period.

Refund requests submitted after 30 days are reviewed on a case-by-case basis and are not guaranteed. Customers who have made more than three refund requests in a 12-month period may be flagged for manual review.

ORDER STATUS

Orders are typically processed within 1 to 2 business days. Standard shipping takes 5 to 7 business days. Express shipping takes 2 to 3 business days.

Customers can track their order using the tracking number sent to their registered email address after dispatch. If a tracking number has not been received within 3 business days of placing an order, customers should contact support.

Orders that have not arrived within 10 business days of the estimated delivery date are considered delayed. In such cases, a replacement or refund will be issued after an investigation is completed with the shipping carrier.

TECHNICAL SUPPORT

Technical support is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM IST. Support requests submitted outside these hours will be responded to on the next business day.

For software or platform issues, customers should include the following details when contacting support: account email, device type, operating system version, and a description of the issue along with any error messages received.

Critical issues affecting core functionality are escalated to the engineering team within 2 hours of being reported. Non-critical issues are addressed within 24 to 48 business hours.

If an issue cannot be resolved within 7 business days, the customer is entitled to a service credit equivalent to one month of their subscription fee.

COMPLAINTS AND ESCALATIONS

Customers who are dissatisfied with the resolution provided by the support team may escalate their complaint to the customer success manager. Escalated complaints are reviewed within 3 business days.

All complaints are logged and reviewed monthly to identify recurring issues. Customers will receive a written acknowledgment of their complaint within 24 hours.

Acme Corp does not tolerate abusive or threatening communication toward support staff. In such cases, Acme Corp reserves the right to terminate the customer relationship.

GENERAL POLICIES

Acme Corp reserves the right to update these policies at any time. Customers will be notified of significant changes via email at least 14 days before the changes take effect.

All support interactions are logged for quality assurance and training purposes. Customer data is handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy and applicable data protection regulations.

For general inquiries not covered by this document, customers may contact support at support@acmecorp.com or through the in-app chat widget.
